/nick - change username | /nick <newUsername>
/join - join an existing stream, if you wish to join a non-existence stream, use /create | /join <streamName>
/leave - leave current stream or another stream you have joined | /leave <OPTIONAL:streamName>
/switch - change streams to chat in another stream | /switch <streamName>
/create - create a brand new stream, if stream already exists, you will just join that stream | /create <streamName>
/list - lists all the streams you have joined | /list
/help - Shows this screen
